How does it work?

Power Your Dreams is an incentive program from Microsoft to reward @gamedigital employees for selling XBOX Series X/S consoles with attachments!

Sell an XBOX Series X/S with:

- XBOX Controllers

-1st Party Software or accessories

-XBOX Game Pass

and you could WIN for your store! The more Series X/S consoles you sell with attachments will earn an individual entries towards prizes for themselves and their store. Entries will contribute towards weekly and monthly prizes which means more winners and more prizes!

From gift cars to hoodies, there's prizes for everyone up for grabs!
